Our Story and Mission

The SorSoap Story: Passion for Organic Soap Making At SorSoap, our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, eco-friendly soaps that are gentle on your skin and the planet. Founded by a team of passionate skincare enthusiasts, SorSoap combines organic ingredients with sustainable practices to create soaps that nourish, hydrate, and rejuvenate. Our Commitment to Sustainability and Ethics As a company, we prioritize sustainability. From the sourcing of our ingredients to the eco-friendly packaging, we strive to make a positive impact on the environment. We believe that what you put on your skin should be pure, natural, and free from harmful chemicals. Handmade Organic Soap for Sensitive Skin Every soap bar we make is crafted by hand, using the finest natural ingredients like organic shea butter, coconut oil, and olive oil. Our soap is designed for all skin types, especially sensitive skin, ensuring that it’s free from harsh chemicals, fragrances, and artificial additives.
